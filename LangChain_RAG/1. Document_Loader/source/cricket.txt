Cricket is one of the most popular sports in the world, played by millions and followed by billions. Originating in England, the game has evolved over centuries and is now played in various formats, including Test cricket, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and the fast-paced Twenty20 (T20) format.

A standard cricket match involves two teams of eleven players each. One team bats while the other bowls and fields, with the objective of scoring runs and dismissing opposing batsmen. The game is played on an oval-shaped field with a 22-yard pitch in the center, where the main action takes place.

Cricket is particularly popular in countries like India, Australia, England, Pakistan, and South Africa, where passionate fans follow their teams closely. Legendary players such as Sachin Tendulkar, Virat Kohli, Don Bradman, and M.S. Dhoni have left an indelible mark on the sport.

With the advent of T20 leagues like the Indian Premier League (IPL), cricket has gained even more global recognition, attracting players from different nations. The game continues to evolve, incorporating new rules and technologies to enhance fairness and excitement.
